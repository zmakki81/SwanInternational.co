Flight Delay Service Terms of use
18 June 2026 1 Min Read

The Flight Delay Service is available to eligible customers who add this service during booking.

How It Works

  • The service monitors the status of your booked flight in real time.
  • If your flight's departure is delayed beyond the minimum qualifying period stated in your booking confirmation, you may become eligible for the associated benefit.
  • Flight status is sourced from recognized flight data providers.

Eligibility

  • The service applies only to the flight itinerary linked to the booking reference provided at purchase.
  • Valid for the specific journey for which it was purchased.
  • Customers must provide accurate booking and contact details.

Service Benefit

  • Where a qualifying delay occurs, the applicable benefit is processed in line with the parameters communicated at the time of purchase.
  • Benefit eligibility is determined using actual recorded flight data.

Exclusions

The service does not apply where:

  • Incorrect booking details have been provided.
  • The customer voluntarily changes, cancels, or does not board the flight.
  • The delay cannot be verified through official flight records.
  • Duplicate bookings have been made for the same traveler and journey.
